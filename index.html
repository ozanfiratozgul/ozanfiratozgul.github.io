I am a doctoral student in Maastricht University. I am mostly focused on designing data processing pipelines for cardiological signals. Before that, I was working as a data scientist in Big Data Group of STM (Ankara). Here, we propose AI solutions to our customers in various industrial domains. In this role, my main tasks include (but not limited to) sensor data processing, spectral analyses and statistical/machine learning-based time series anomaly detection pipelines. I have a special interest in adapting neural networks to solve complicated signal processing problems.

Lately, I have become concerned in blog keeping on technical and on some other peculiar issues.
