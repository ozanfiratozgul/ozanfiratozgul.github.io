I am working as a data scientist in Big Data Group of STM (Ankara). Here, we propose machine learning-inspired solutions to our customers in various industrial domains. In this role, my main tasks include (but not limited to) sensor data processing, spectral analyses and statistical/machine learning-based time series anomaly detection pipelines. I have a special interest in modifying neural network for solving complicated signal processing problems.

Lately, I have become concerned in blog keeping on technical and philosophical issues.
